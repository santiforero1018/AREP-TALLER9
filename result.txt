Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional soccer player widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of
all time. He was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal. Ronaldo has played for several top clubs during his
career, including Sporting Lisbon, Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. Known for his incredible athleticism,
skill, and goal-scoring prowess, he has won numerous awards throughout his career, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or
awards, which are given annually to the world's best player. Ronaldo is celebrated for his speed, agility, powerful
shot, and remarkable ability in the air, particularly his heading. Off the field, he's known for his philanthropy,
business ventures, and global celebrity status.
